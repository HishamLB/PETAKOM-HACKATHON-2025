About

The Pekan Waterfront is a scenic recreational area along the Pahang River. This beautifully landscaped promenade offers stunning views of the river and serves as a popular gathering spot for locals and tourists alike.

Visitors can enjoy peaceful walks, sample local delicacies at the food stalls, or take river cruises to explore the area from a different perspective. The waterfront is particularly beautiful during sunset when the sky reflects on the river's surface.
